Hello, I'm Natalie

I'm a life-long writer and storyteller with a passion for creating immersive worlds and complex characters. My journey spans across marketing and communications, news and media, video and photography — all of which inform my writing.

My debut novel, Ghost Train, is out now and has been praised for its haunting narrative and rich character development. I believe that stories have the power to transform, heal, and connect us all.

When I'm not writing, you can find me exploring nature, sipping coffee in quaint bookshops, or conducting writing workshops. I'm currently working on my next novel, set to release in late 2025.
